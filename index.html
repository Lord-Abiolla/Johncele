    Work injury benefits Acts(WIBA)

    Provides cover against accidental injury/death arising out of and in the course of duty. This is a cover for occupational risks.

    Benefits include:

    • Death - 8 years earnings
    • Permanent Total Disablement - 8 years earnings
    • Temporary Total Disablement - weekly earnings up to 104 weeks
    • Medical expenses - Kshs. 100,000
    • Funeral expenses - Kshs. 30,000
